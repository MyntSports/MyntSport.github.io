Make Sports a Family Activity

MyntSports provides fun curriculum for parents to spend quality time with
their children while teaching them the fundamentals of a variety of sports.

 

Our goal is to form a community of like-minded parents who see the great
bonding opportunity that developing a child's love for sport can provide.
